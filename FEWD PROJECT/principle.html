WOMEN EMPOWERMENT PRINCIPLES

2005

PRINCIPLE-1

Establish high-level corporate leadership for
gender equality.


PRINCIPLE-2

Treat all women and men fairly at work with respect
and support human rights and nondiscrimination
at all cost.

PRINCIPLE-3

Ensure the health and well being of all women and
men workers.


PRINCIPLE-4

Promote education,training and proffesional
development for women.

2006

PRINCIPLE-5

Implement enterprize development,supply chain and
marketing practices that empower women.


PRINCIPLE-6

Promote equality through community initiatives and
advocacy.

PRINCIPLE-7

Measure and publicly report on progress to achieve
gender equality.
